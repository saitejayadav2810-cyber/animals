🟢 Milch Breeds (Indian)

Sahiwal
Also: Lola, Lambi Bar, Montgomery
Milch
OriginSahiwal dist., Montgomery, Pakistan (reared widely in Punjab, UP, Delhi)
ColourReddish-dun to pale red with white patches; sometimes dark red
HornsShort, stumpy (~3 inches); thick base
Milk1800–2100 kg/lactation (best Indian milch); 4.5% fat
Key FactBest Indian milch breed; tolerant of tick infestation; temperament – docile
🧠 MEMORY TRICKTOP milcher of India – Sahiwal = SA for SAARE ANIMALS se zyada doodh
Red Sindhi
Also: Red Karachi, Sindhi, Mahi
Milch
OriginKarachi & Hyderabad (Sindh), Pakistan; found in Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Odisha
ColourDistinctly RED – shades from dark red to pale red/yellow
HornsStumpy and short, curving outward
Milk1700+ kg/lactation; 4.8–5% fat
Key FactHigh heat tolerance, tick resistance; used in crossbreeding programs globally
🧠 MEMORY TRICKRED dress = RED Sindhi – remember by COLOUR name itself. Smaller than Tharparkar.
Gir
Also: Desan, Bhadwari, Kathiawari, Surati
Milch
OriginGir Forest, South Kathiawar, Gujarat
ColourWhite with dark red/chocolate-brown patches; or plain red
Horns🌙 HALF-MOON shape – curved downward then upward; most distinctive feature
Milk1590–1800 kg/lactation; 4.5% fat; in Brazil: 6000–8000 kg!
Key FactExported to Brazil, Israel, USA. Brazil's Guzerat descended from Gir
🧠 MEMORY TRICKGIR = Girar (curve) horns → Half-moon curved horns + Gir Forest, Gujarat. Famous globally.
Tharparkar
Also: White Sindhi, Grey Sindhi, Thari
MilchDual
OriginTharparkar dist., Sindh, Pakistan; Thar Desert (Rajasthan, Gujarat)
ColourWhite or light grey; "face and socks white"
HornsLyre-shaped (curving upward and inward); medium length
Milk1663 kg/lactation; survives on Sewan grass alone in desert
Key FactDual purpose; drought/heat hardy; "Grey Sindhi" nickname
🧠 MEMORY TRICKTHAR = Thar desert, white like desert sand, LYRE horns like a musical instrument 🎵
Rathi
Also: Rath
MilchDual
OriginBikaner, Churu, Nagaur, Ganganagar – Western Rajasthan
ColourBrown with white patches; or white with brown patches
Milk1100–1800 kg/lactation; 4.9% fat; drought tolerant
Key FactCalled "Sahiwal of Rajasthan"; descends from Sahiwal × local breeds
🧠 MEMORY TRICKRATHI = Rajasthan ki Sahiwal – 5th milch breed, often forgotten. Western Rajasthan origin.

Also: HF; Black and White
MilchForeign
OriginNetherlands (Holland/Friesland); now worldwide
ColourBLACK and WHITE patches – large, irregular; most recognizable dairy cow
Milk6000–9000 kg/lactation; 3.5–3.7% fat (low fat)
Key FactHighest milk-producing breed in the world; used in Frieswal (HF × Sahiwal)

Jersey
MilchForeign
OriginIsle of Jersey, Channel Islands (UK)
ColourFawn/tan to dark brown; large DEER-like eyes; black muzzle

🧠 MEMORY TRICKJERSEY = J for JAM (cream-rich); smallest body, HIGHEST fat; deer-eyed beauty

Milk5000–7000 kg/lactation; 4% fat; high protein

🧠 MEMORY TRICKBrown Swiss = Brown SWISS chocolate = steady, old, reliable; mountain hardy breed
